QHeaderBar

Inherits from QFrame

Overview

The main control area of an application window, where there are close, maximize and minimize buttons, along with a movement area and the application icon.

It's a regular widget and can be aligned wherever you want, not just at the top of the window, but also in other places like the middle or bottom if you're a psychopath.
